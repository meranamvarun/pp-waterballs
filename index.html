About PP Waterballs

PP Waterballs, formerly known as "Prem Prakash Golgappa", was created by two friends, Prem and Prakash. They became an instant hit among the crowds in Delhi with their delicious pani puri.

Our journey took an unexpected turn when our founders overheard a conversation of Mr. Jaspal Bhatti, a CA and currently the financial brain behind PP Waterballs, about starting a public company. This led to the revolutionary idea of expanding our business by converting it into a public company and changing our name to "PP Waterballs".
